  • SQI College of ICT

More About SQI

SQI College of ICT is a specialised tertiary institution focused on information & communication technology (ICT) and allied tech‐fields.

The institution has recently attained monotechnic status (approved by the National Board for Technical Education, NBTE) so it can offer National Diploma (ND) programmes and other diplomas.

  • June 12 Cultural Center

More About June 12

It is owned/operated by the Ogun State Ministry of Culture and Tourism and serves as a multi-purpose cultural and event venue.

It was named in honour of the significance of June 12, 1993, the pivotal presidential election year associated with Moshood Kashimawo Olawale Abiola, a native of Abeokuta.

  • OOPL Zoo

What to See & Features

The park houses over 250 species of animals—both indigenous and exotic—including lions, crocodiles, monkeys/apes, ostriches, camels, etc.

The zoo forms part of the larger OOPL complex that also features a museum, hotel resort, amusement park and other amenities.
